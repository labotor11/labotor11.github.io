about me

My name is Linna Praisa, and I am an Interactive Designer graduate that has worked as a Graphic Designer/Creative Developer for 4 years, with a small amount of experience with collaborating on Front-End and Adbanner projects - usually aiding with HTML/CSS and GSAP animations. Currently based in Utrecht, The Netherlands.

I am also passionately creative on the side, with drawing & illustration being my main hobby since childhood - and have picked up a recent interest in 3D modeling, sculpting and texturing.

my work

Projects from my time as a Creative Developer at Mr Ynk with some freelance work in-between. Mostly website/banner/campaigngame design, but also some more illustrative/concepting work is shown here.

My longest standing hobby - been drawing since i've been able to pick up a pencil! Only digital work is shown here as it's the medium in which I tend to create fully finished works, but I do keep a few traditional sketchbooks. I tend to gravitate towards character & creature art - drawing mostly inspiration various fantasy settings and folklore.

A somewhat recent hobby - only really started to get hands-on with Blender in 2023. Most previous experience in modeling and texturing came from working on various game mods.
